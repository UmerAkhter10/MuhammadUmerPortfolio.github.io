Design and Making of Line Follower Robot

Concept of working of line follower is related to light. The behavior of light at black and white surface is utilized in this project. When light fall on a white surface it is almost full reflected and in case of black surface light is completely absorbed. This behavior of light is used in building a line follower robot. The whole line follower robot can be divided into 3 parts:
Sensor Part:
This section contains IR diodes, potentiometer, Comparator (Op-Amp LM-324) and LED’s. Potentiometer is used for setting reference voltage at comparator’s one terminal and IR sensors are used to sense the line and provide a change in voltage at comparator’s second terminal. Then comparator compares both voltages and generates a digital signal at output. Here in this line follower circuit we have used two comparators for two sensors. LM 324 is used as comparator. LM324 has inbuilt four low noise Op-amps.
Control Part:
Arduino UNO is used for controlling whole the process of line follower robot. The outputs of comparators are connected to digital pin number 2, 3, 4 & 5 of Arduino. Arduino read these signals and send commands to driver circuit (Motor driver IC) to drive line follower.
Driver Part:
Driver part consists motor driver IC(L-298) and two DC motors. Motor driver is used for driving motors because Arduino does not supply enough voltage and current to motor. So we add a motor driver circuit to get enough voltage and current for motor. Arduino sends commands to this motor driver and then it drive motors.
