Face Recognition Algorithm

Face Recognition System, as a replacement of obsolete biometric identification technologies, is a break though in the field of security and surveillance. Higher interclass similarity and large number of classes/persons are the major challenges that adversely affect the performance of a face recognition system. MTCNN (Multitask Cascade Convolutional Neural Network), FaceNet and Boyer-Moore Majority Voting Algorithm are combined to deal with high flux density of data at peak hours. System is tested with online available images dataset of 200 persons, 97% test accuracy is achieved using this technique.
