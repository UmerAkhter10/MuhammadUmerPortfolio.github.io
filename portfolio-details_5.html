Visualization and Analysis of HR Data

For HR manager it is inevitable to keep an eye on HR data. This Power BI dashboard designed to render full insight into HR data. Data is first cleaned in Power Query to remove any anomaly.
The main parameters highlighted in dashboard are,

  • Total Employees (Male and Female Employees)
  • Number of employees not due for promotion
  • Number of employees due for promotion
  • Number of employees vs. Years of Service
  • Number of Employees vs. Job Level
  • Number of Employees vs. Distance travelled to workplace
  • Details and Actions Tabs are provided for further visualizations
This dashboard will assist HR Manager to make data-driven decisions.
