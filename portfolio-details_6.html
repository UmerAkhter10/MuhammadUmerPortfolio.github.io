Analysis and Insights Extractions from Sales Data

Dashboard provide spontaneous insight to data, where as detailed analysis is done while making report. A multinational home appliance selling company is continuously observing declined profit for last five years and obviously wants to find the reason behind.
The higher-ups also have some question like what is best place/country to open a new store. To solve these queries detailed analysis is performed on data and findings are provided in the form of report.
Main findings are,

  • Percentage Profit of different item is not equal.
  • Higher sales of low percentage profit items decreases average profit.
  • India has highest profit-per-store that why it is the best place to open new store.
  • Actual price is increasing over the time but list price at which items are sold are consistent that makes profit decreasing.
  • Data has proven that from 2014 to 2018 profit has decreasing trend.
  • To keep company profitable and progressive list price (label price) has to increase.
