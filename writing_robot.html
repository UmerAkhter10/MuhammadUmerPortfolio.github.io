Design and Fabrication of 2D Writing Robot

Diagrams and graphs are the tools for visualizing, the data patterns, trends and comparisons. And, a graphical hardcopy is often the preferred output from a digital processing system. A drawing machine could be device to get output design. This machine differs from Inkjet as well as LaserJet printers because it draws a continuous line, just like a pen on paper. CNC is used to control the function of motors that ultimately control the pen motion.
Two Stepper motors and one Servo Motors are used to precisely control the movement of pen holder in fine discrete steps.
Writing Machine is normally controlled through a set of extensions of Inkscape, the popular and free vector graphics program. Basic operation is much like that of a printer driver: you import or make a drawing in Inkscape, generate G-Code of vector and use the extensions to plot your text or artwork.
The machine can be used for

  • PCB Printing,
  • Making Formal Invitations,
  • Technical Drawing,
  • Thankyou Notes and Cards
After some proper modifications it can be converted into an Engraving machine, Hot-wire foam cutters, Plasma cutters, Water jet cutters, Laser cutting machine, Knife cutting, Glass cutting or a 3D Printer.
